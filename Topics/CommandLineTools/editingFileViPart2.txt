Editing Files with vi Part 2

vi commands - his includes things like searching and navigating text, 
appending and deleting, and saving changes.

i insert is before
a append is after

cc - change the line
cw - changes the word

dd - Deletes a line
dw - Delete word

D - Deletes the line after the cursor

y - Yakes the line (cut)
V - Yank multiple line, move the cursor to the end of where to cut or delete. The press y to yank.

p - puts the line back (paste)

q! - Quit without changes
w! - Overwrite the file

:e - Change to another file
:e! - Change to another file without saving
